About

I’m a linguistics/NLP student in Paris. I build clean web interfaces and language tools. I value simple architecture, quick iteration, and legible UX.

Originally from Iran, I studied English Didactics for my bachelor’s degree and have years of experience teaching languages. This background shapes how I think about technology, learning, and communication.

Today, I’m focused on computational linguistics and building AI-powered language learning tools. My main project is a large web app that integrates LLMs, TTS, and custom NLP pipelines. It’s already over 15k lines of code and follows Clean Architecture principles.

Outside of coding, I explore photography, French language learning, and writing. These keep me grounded and help me see technology as part of a broader creative life.

“Make it simple. Then make it obvious.”

— also my coding philosophy
