Chronically Waitlisted: An Underdog Story

2026-01-20 · Bulac - 2026

I probably should be throwing my keyboard across my room right now, but honestly? I’m just numb to it.

"Under-achiever"

This label has been haunting me lately. Last year, I was exactly 2nd on the waitlist for the program I actually wanted. This year? I delivered an infinitely better application, leveled up my technical chops, and somehow clinched the highly coveted, utterly useless spot of... 1st on the waitlist. Progress, right?(It's an implication and not an elliciatation(fuck thesis))

It’s been nearly 8 years since I was forced to study teaching at 18. I essentially signed a contract with the devil - aka known as Farhangian University.

"Underdog"

I’ll probably always hold a grudge against my father for that detour. He used some next-level dark psychology to make me settle for less and temporarily derailed my life path.

But here’s the plot twist: surviving that taught me how to grind. I’m done settling. I'm pivoting hard into building real products, and I refuse to quit. The world is mine for the taking, but to hit actual financial independence, I know I've got to get my hands dirty and execute. Let the games begin. be ghole Poria, Next wine.
